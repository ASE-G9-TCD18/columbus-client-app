Welcome to Columbus

The Columbus app helps you custom tailor your commutes with fellow travellers you like.

What is Columbus?

Columbus is a platform which allows users to create and join groups for their frequent travels and commutes

What do I do with Columbus?

You can join groups and create groups with nearby friends for daily commute or an instant journey plans. You can co-ordinate, manage and plan your route, making the journey worth remembering.
